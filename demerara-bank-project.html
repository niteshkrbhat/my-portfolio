Transforming traditional banking operations into a seamless digital experience through an intuitive, secure, and customer-focused online banking platform for retail and corporate users.


Demerara Bank serves a wide range of personal and business banking customers across Guyana, offering services such as accounts, loans, credit cards, transfers, payments, and online banking solutions. As digital banking adoption continues to grow, customers increasingly expect fast, secure, and accessible financial services that work seamlessly across devices.


The challenge was to redesign the digital banking experience into a more intuitive and modern platform that simplifies complex financial tasks while maintaining trust, security, and compliance. Existing banking workflows often feel outdated, difficult to navigate, and overwhelming for users performing routine activities like transfers, bill payments, account monitoring, or managing business transactions.

Description

Demerara Bank is a modern banking platform offering secure and customer-focused financial services including digital banking, loans, online transactions, and business banking solutions. The platform is designed to provide a seamless and accessible banking experience through intuitive digital interfaces and simplified financial services.

As a UI/UX Designer, I was responsible for designing the end-to-end digital banking experience, including user flows, wireframes, dashboards, responsive layouts, and scalable design systems. The focus was on creating a secure, intuitive, and modern banking interface that simplifies financial interactions while improving usability and customer engagement across web and mobile platforms.
