FV Bank

FV Bank redefines online banking by merging traditional and crypto transactions into a single seamless platform. For the mobile UI/UX, I focused on crafting a dual-mode experience—light and dark themes designed for different moods and environments. The design emphasizes clarity, security, and modernity, featuring intuitive dashboards, quick-transfer gestures, and real-time transaction updates. Every interaction was designed to feel fluid and professional, making digital banking both elegant and effortless across fiat and crypto worlds.

Ridewise

Ridewise is a next-generation rental and ride-sharing platform inspired by Blusmart’s eco-friendly model. My UI/UX approach centered on simplicity and speed—making booking rides a two-tap experience. The interface uses clean navigation, live tracking visuals, and dynamic fare cards for a smooth flow. With bold typography, bright call-to-actions, and responsive map animations, I designed the experience to feel smart, sustainable, and city-ready, empowering users to travel efficiently with confidence.

The flow ensures crypto management feels personal yet protected, making Yug Wallet a modern evolution of secure digital ownership.
