Transforming fragmented enterprise data operations into a scalable, domain-driven, and AI-powered platform experience.


Modern enterprises struggled to operationalize distributed data ecosystems across teams, domains, and cloud environments. Data workflows were fragmented, governance lacked consistency, and business domains remained heavily dependent on centralized engineering teams to access, manage, and operationalize data products.


Symbiontra was conceptualized as a unified operational experience platform for modern Data Mesh ecosystems — enabling organizations to procure, produce, publish, govern, and consume Data-Products-as-a-Service (DPaaS) through a centralized yet domain-driven platform experience.

My Role
Sole Product Designer
Responsibilities & Deliverables
UX Strategy, Product Thinking, User Research, Information Architecture, Workflow Design, Interaction Design, Prompt UX, Design System, Prototyping
Timeline
2024, 8-10 Weeks

Description

The product focused on simplifying complex enterprise data interactions through AI-assisted workflows, low-code/no-code operational tooling, and scalable multi-tenant architecture.

The UX challenge was to design a platform that could make highly technical data operations feel intuitive, collaborative, and operationally transparent for both business and engineering teams while supporting enterprise-scale governance and distributed ownership models.
