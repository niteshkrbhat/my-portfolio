Transforming complex business data into real-time, actionable insights through customizable, AI-driven dashboards built for Odoo enterprises.


Businesses using Odoo often struggle with scattered reporting systems, static analytics, and limited visibility across departments. Teams rely on multiple tools to monitor sales, finance, inventory, CRM, and operations — making decision-making slower and less efficient.


Dashboard Ninja was designed to solve this challenge by creating a centralized analytics experience where users can build dynamic dashboards, monitor KPIs in real time, and generate meaningful visual reports without technical complexity. The platform combines flexibility, AI-powered automation, and advanced customization to help businesses make faster, data-driven decisions.

Description

The product enables users to generate complete dashboards using AI, customize layouts with drag-and-drop interactions, create interactive visualizations, apply advanced filters, and access business-critical metrics from a single interface. Dashboard Ninja also supports real-time dashboards, multi-company environments, drill-down analytics, responsive layouts, and exportable reports — making enterprise reporting significantly more scalable and user-friendly.

The goal of the product was to design a scalable business intelligence experience for Odoo users — simplifying reporting workflows, improving operational visibility, and empowering organizations to make smarter decisions with confidence.
